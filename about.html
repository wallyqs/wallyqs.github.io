About

I'm a Tokyo based full stack developer specializing in PaaS & logging technologies, as well as declarative approaches around the deployment of workloads.

In the past, I have participated in communities such as Google Summer of Code and the Recurse Center (formerly HackerSchool).

Originally from Guadalajara, Mexico (also known as the Engineering Hub from Mexico)

In October 2010, I moved to Tokyo to join Rakuten during its initial stages of globalization as part of the Rakuten Research team, where I had my first experience working in a completely Japanese environment.

Then in 2012, I moved on as one of the first members of Rakuten PaaS team to help in its launch. It was a Cloudfoundry based solution, and we needed persistent logs so I implemented its logging infrastructure using Fluentd.

I am proficient in Japanese (JLPT 2 - Business level), English & Spanish.

My editor is Emacs. Also, I really, really like Org mode, so if support in Github is broken please ping me.
